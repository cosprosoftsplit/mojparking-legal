Privacy Policy

Moj eParking — Last updated: May 13, 2026

Introduction

Cosmic Production d.o.o. ("we", "us", or "our"), a company registered in Split, Croatia, operates the Moj eParking mobile application ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Data We Handle Locally

Moj eParking is designed to keep parking information on your device. The app may store the parking location you save, notes, floor or zone details, timer settings, and an optional parking photo locally on your device so you can find your vehicle again.

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers.
  • We do not collect analytics or usage data.
  • We do not operate a server that receives your saved parking locations, notes, or photos.
  • We do not sell your information.

Location Permission

The App requests when-in-use location permission only so you can save the location of your parked vehicle and navigate back to it. Location is requested when you use parking features in the App. Saved parking locations remain on your device unless you choose to remove the App or delete the saved parking entry.

Photos

If you add a parking photo, the photo is stored locally as part of your saved parking entry. We do not upload parking photos to our servers.

Advertising and Consent

The free version of the App may show ads using Google AdMob. Google may process device, advertising, and consent signals to provide and measure ads, subject to your consent choices and applicable law. Where required, the App uses Google's User Messaging Platform to request and manage advertising consent. On iOS, the App may also request Apple's App Tracking Transparency permission before personalized ad tracking is used.

You can manage ad privacy choices from the App's Settings screen where available. You can also change Apple's tracking permission in iOS Settings.

In-App Purchases

The App uses Apple's StoreKit framework for optional in-app purchases. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how Apple handles payment data.

Data Storage

All parking entries and preferences controlled by the App are stored locally on your device. If you delete the App, locally stored App data is removed according to iOS behavior. Apple and Google may separately process information for App Store purchases, ads, or consent systems as described in their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Advertising consent settings are configured not to tag users as under the age of consent by default; if you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

GDPR Compliance

As a company based in the European Union (Croatia), we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Most parking data is stored locally on your device and can be deleted by deleting entries or removing the App. For advertising consent and ad-related processing, you can manage choices in the App where available, iOS Settings, and Google's consent interfaces. If you have any questions regarding your data rights, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
