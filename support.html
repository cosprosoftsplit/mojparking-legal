Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Open Moj eParking, allow location access when prompted, and tap the save parking button when you park. You can then navigate back to the saved location from the main screen.

What premium features are available?

Premium unlocks parking timers with notifications, parking photos, parking history, the home screen widget, and an ad-free experience. Once purchased, premium features are yours to keep permanently.

Why does the app ask for location?

The app uses when-in-use location access to save the spot where your vehicle is parked. Saved parking locations remain on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.

Can the app pay for my parking?

No. Moj eParking is a parking-location and reminder utility. It does not pay parking fees, verify local rules, or guarantee that a parking spot is legal or available.

How do I restore my purchase?

If you have previously purchased a premium upgrade and need to restore it (for example, after reinstalling the App or switching devices), open the App and tap "Restore Purchases" in the settings or premium section. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase. The restoration process is handled by Apple and requires an active internet connection.

How do I request a refund?

All purchases are processed by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID. Select the purchase and follow the instructions to submit your refund request.

Is my data private?

Parking entries, notes, and optional photos are stored locally on your device. The free version may show ads through Google AdMob, and ad consent choices can be managed in the app where available.
